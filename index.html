Your two-year arc

Phase 1

Learn

Months 1–6

Observe everything. What sells? Who stops? Don't optimize yet — just notice.

Phase 2

Adjust

Months 7–18

Use what you learned. Change one thing at a time so you know what worked.

Phase 3

Decide

Months 19–24

You have real data now. Make an informed decision about your next chapter.
