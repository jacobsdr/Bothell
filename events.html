4–7

Pop Shops on Main — Opening Weekend

Thu–Sun · Noon to 6:00 PM · Main Street, Downtown Bothell

Regular operating weekend. Expect moderate foot traffic from nearby Farmers Market on Saturday. Good weekend to post your opening announcement if you haven't already.

Market Day

Jun

5

Food Truck Activation

Friday · Noon to 3:00 PM · Pop Shops Lot

Rotating food truck in the shared open space. Brings additional lunchtime foot traffic through the lot. Post on social Thursday evening to capture people planning their lunch.

Food Truck

Jun

6

Bothell Saturday Farmers Market

Saturday · 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM · Sammamish River Trail area

Weekly Farmers Market draws significant foot traffic to the area. One of your highest-traffic Saturdays. Make sure your space is fully set up by 10:00 AM and your exterior chalkboard is out.

City Event

Jun

11

Vendor Check-In — Program Coordinator

Thursday · During open hours · Your unit

Monthly informal check-in with the Pop Shops coordinator. Good time to flag any space issues, ask questions, or share what's been working. Not mandatory but encouraged — especially for newer vendors.

Program

Jun

19–20

Downtown Bothell Summer Kick-Off

Fri–Sat · Downtown Bothell

City-organized event welcoming the start of summer in downtown Bothell. Historically one of the highest-traffic weekends of the year for the Pop Shops lot. Plan extra inventory, make sure your space is refreshed, and promote heavily on social Wednesday–Friday.

City Event

Jun

21

Summer Solstice

Sunday · All day

Longest day of the year — great content hook for social posts. "Summer officially starts and so does our busiest season" is a natural post for Instagram or Facebook. Good day for a display refresh to mark the season change.

City Event

Jun

25

Pop Shops Photo Day

Thursday · During open hours · All units

The city will be taking photos of the Pop Shops area for use on the city website and social channels. Make sure your space looks its best — clean display, hero product prominent, exterior signage out. The city may tag you in posts afterward.
